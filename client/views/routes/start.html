Welcome!

inOrder is a fun trivia game based on sorting five
randomly placed answers into the proper order.

Click to view instructions

First, choose a category from the list.

Next, properly sort the five answers for each question to earn points.
Use your mouse to drag-and-drop the answers into the order you want.
The order should always be increasing (going from top to bottom).
Each question has a difficulty level and number of points you can earn.
You will lose 50 points for each attempt made. When you are out of points,
you will be out of attempts. You will see the correct order, and move to the
next question...so choose your order carefully!

Then, when you have successfully ordered the last question in the chosen category,
you will have completed that category,earning a badge for your effort!
View your points total and you can choose a new category.
